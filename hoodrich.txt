Pennix started pursuing a rap career in 2014 after he released a song titled "Go Get Money" and featured on a Peewee Longway track titled "African Diamonds".
He released a string of mixtapes during 2015 to 2017, and was later signed to Gucci Mane's label 1017 Records.
He gained attention with his track "We Don't Luv Em".
In 2020, Pennix was arrested on RICO charges.
In 2022, he was sentenced to 5 years in prison, from 15 years prior to a plea bargain.
He was released on October 19th 2025.
